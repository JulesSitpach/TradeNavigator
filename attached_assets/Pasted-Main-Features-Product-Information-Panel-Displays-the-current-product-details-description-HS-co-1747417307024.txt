Main Features
Product Information Panel

Displays the current product details (description, HS code, category)
Shows origin and destination countries
Indicates product value and quantity
Landed Cost Calculation

Breaks down the total landed cost into component parts
Shows product value, shipping costs, insurance, duties, taxes, and other fees
Calculates the final landed cost per unit and in total
Duty Rate Information

Displays the applicable duty rate based on HS code, origin, and destination
Shows any applicable trade agreements or preferential rates
Explains how the duty amount was calculated
Tax Calculation

Calculates VAT, GST, or sales tax based on destination country
Shows tax rates and amounts
Explains taxation thresholds and exemptions if applicable
Shipping Cost Analysis

Compares different shipping methods (air, sea, rail, road)
Shows estimated transit times and costs for each method
Highlights the recommended shipping method based on cost/time preferences
Additional Fees Breakdown

Lists customs processing fees
Shows documentation and filing fees
Includes handling, insurance, and other miscellaneous charges
Visualization Elements

Pie chart showing the proportion of each cost component
Bar chart comparing different shipping methods
Cost trend analysis over time for recurring shipments
Cost Optimization Tips

Provides actionable recommendations to reduce costs
Suggests potential duty savings strategies
Highlights areas where costs could be optimized
Key Benefits for SMBs
Complete Transparency: Shows every cost component with no hidden fees
Predictable Budgeting: Allows businesses to accurately forecast the total landed cost
Cost Comparison: Enables side-by-side comparison of different shipping scenarios
Decision Support: Helps make informed decisions about product sourcing and shipping
Educational Element: Explains complex duty and tax calculations in simple terms
The Cost Breakdown Dashboard serves as the foundation for the other specialized dashboards, providing the core data that powers the Tariff Analysis, Regulations, and Alternative Routes views. It helps small and medium businesses understand their true cost of international trade and make data-driven decisions about their shipping strategies.
