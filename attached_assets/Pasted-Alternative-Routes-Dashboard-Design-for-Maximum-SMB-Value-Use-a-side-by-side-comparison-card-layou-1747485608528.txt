Alternative Routes Dashboard
Design for Maximum SMB Value:

Use a side-by-side comparison card layout showing each route option
Include a visual timeline showing transit duration with key milestones
Highlight the total cost difference between options in large, bold text
Add risk indicators (low/medium/high) with explanatory tooltips
Include a "Recommended Option" flag based on the business's specific needs
Show cash flow impact visualization to help SMBs understand working capital implications

Benefits:

Provides clear cost-benefit analysis for decision-making
Helps businesses understand the trade-offs between speed and cost
Reduces risk through visibility of shipping reliability differences
Enables better cash flow management and inventory planning

Drawbacks:

May require frequent updates as shipping rates change
Risk indicators may oversimplify complex logistics realities
Recommendations may not account for all business-specific factors

Considerations:

Include ability to adjust importance of factors (cost vs. speed vs. reliability)
Consider integration with inventory management systems for better forecasting
Allow for seasonal variation in shipping options and rates

Tariff Analysis Dashboard
Design for Maximum SMB Value:

Create a "Duty Rate Comparison" section with visual scales showing relative rates
Include a "Savings Opportunities" panel highlighting specific classification strategies
Add a tariff timeline showing historical changes and future scheduled changes
Design an interactive map showing duty rates for the same product across different markets
Include actionable insights in callout boxes rather than raw data tables
Add document templates or guides for tariff classification disputes

Benefits:

Identifies immediate savings opportunities through proper classification
Provides market expansion insights based on favorable tariff rates
Helps businesses anticipate and prepare for tariff changes
Reduces risk of misclassification penalties

Drawbacks:

Complex tariff rules may require legal expertise beyond dashboard guidance
Suggested classifications need careful review for compliance
Historical data may not always predict future tariff changes reliably

Considerations:

Include confidence ratings for alternative classification suggestions
Provide access to binding ruling information or similar precedents
Consider integration with product development to design for favorable tariff treatment
